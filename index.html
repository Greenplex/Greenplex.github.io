About Us

We define a complex as a cluster of facilities or structures built for a common purpose. This could be a residential or resort complex, a manufacturing or industrial complex or even a medical or other type of services complex.

Contact

Mission

So how does a complex qualify as a green complex or greenplex? By focusing on our core Greenplex principles:

Sustainability

Treat and manage the entire complex as an ecosystem and allow the businesses and units within the complex to share common resources efficiently.

Environment

Eliminate or minimize any negative impacts on the environment by reusing by-products and recycling waste at every opportunity.

Community

Engage with and provide benefits to local communities including district heat and energy as well as training and employment.

  • Waste to Energy: Miracube

    Hanki Industrial’s most advanced and versatile technology, designed in South Korea after 30 years of optimizing waste to energy technologies.

    • State-of-the-art low temperature pyrolysis system with 25 new patents approved
    • Versatile for different feedstock featuring automated batch control by feedstock characteristics
    • Processes municipal solid waste (MSW), medical waste, specific risk materials and biomass with lowest available maintenance cost
    • 1st plant was commissioned in June 2016 in Seoul, Korea; Input: 100 ton/day Medical Waste and MSW; Output: 30 ton/hour Steam (sold for US$30/ton)
    • 4 confirmed orders in Korea, multiple orders in rest of the world, 1st Canadian order pending approval in British Columbia

  • Odour and VOC Removal: BIO-CAT

    BIO-CAT: Hanki Industrial’s best in class deodorizer with highly proven functionality and diverse applications.

    • State of the art odour eliminating technology
    • Microbial deodorizer that handles odour and Volatile Organic Compounds (VOC) from large scale waste water treatment plants and other installations
    • Oxidizes micro-organisms and decomposes VOCs
    • Optimized removal efficiency enabled by selecting micro-organism according to materials generating odour/VOC
    • Applications: Wastewater, sewerage, food waste treatment facilities, fish/meat processing facilities, manufacturing of solvents/adherents/synthetic fibres & plastic manufacturing process
    • 70+ installations currently operating

  • Wastewater: Hanki Industrial Technology

    Hanki Industrial leads South Korea in designing, constructing, and operating the most efficient waste water treatment facilities across a variety of sectors. Technologies employed: SWRO (seawater reverse osmosis), nano-filter membrane, microbial deodorization.

    • Advanced process that removed nitrogen, phosphorus and eutrophication
    • Established systems with applications in pulp and paper, livestock manure, wastewater from invineration, leachate and landfills
    • Oxidizes micro-organisms and decomposes VOCs

    • Reduced cost as a result of optimized process (based on proven effluent results)
